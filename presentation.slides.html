Investigation of the Twitter Account WeRateDogs

Investigation Overview¶

In this project, I gathered tweet data from different a variety of sources for the Twitter Account WeRateDogs. The goal is to analyze different characteristics for their influence towards a tweet going viral.

Dataset Overview¶

The cleaned project consists of two DataFrames. One containing information about the popularity of a tweet and the other containg a prediction for the dog breed displayed in the image. Both DataFrames hold information on 1.500+ unique tweets.

              color = base_color, order=order)

plt.xlabel('proportion')
plt.title('relative proportion of top 10 dog types');

The golden retriever is the by far most tweeted tog breed. Nearly 8 percent of all tweets depict one. Second is the golden retrievers close relative - the labrador retriever, closely followed by the Pembroke.

Puppers are with a share of 60 percent the most tweeted dog stage, followed by doggos with around 25 percent. Puppos and floofers are rare with each one making up less than 10 percent of all tweets.

First of all, the scatter plot indicates a positive correlation between retweet- and favorite count, meaning that a more favorited tweet is likely to also have a higher retweet count.
